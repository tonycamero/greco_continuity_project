# Community Builder Market Map

## Strategic Premise

The greenfield audience is not young monetary reformers. It is community builders who are already trying to solve trust, contribution, ownership, governance, and value-flow problems.

## Strongest Hidden Segment

The strongest greenfield is **creator and community operators** because they are already asking:

- How do I own my audience?
- How do I own the relationship?
- How do I reward contribution?
- How do I monetize community without extraction?
- How do I avoid being captured by a platform?

Those are not "money reform" questions, but they become exchange questions.

## Strongest Builder Segment

The strongest builder segment is **regenerative public-goods infrastructure**: Greenpill, ReFi DAO, Funding the Commons, Gitcoin-adjacent networks, open-source sustainability groups, and local-first builders.

They already have the vocabulary of coordination, public goods, identity, reputation, and governance. Chapter 21 adds the exchange theory.
